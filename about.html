UniWar Damage Calculator

About

Simple tool for calculating probability distribution of attacks in the UniWar game.
The UniWar game and all artwork are properties of Spooky House Studios
Used in this calculator with kind permission of Spooky House Studios

Variants

  • Desktop version
  • Experimental version - supports calculation of attack sequences
